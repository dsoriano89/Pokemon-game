Pokemon Card Game

  • Pikachu #001
    • Electric

    Pikachu is yellow with brown stripes on its back, black-tipped ears, red electrical pouches on its cheeks, and has a lightning-bolt-shaped tail.

  • Charmander #002
    • Fire

    Charmander is a small, bipedal, dinosaur like Pokémon. Most of its body is colored orange, while its underbelly is a light yellow color.

  • Bulbasaur #003
    • Poison

    Bulbasaur are small, squat amphibian and plant Pokémon that move on all four legs, and have blue-green bodies with darker blue-green spots.

  • Eevee #004
    • Normal

    Eevee is a mammalian creature with brown fur, a bushy tail that has a cream-colored tip, and a furry collar that is also cream-colored.

  • Gengar #005
    • Ghost

    Mewtwo is an artificial Pokémon. It is a bipedal, humanoid Pokémon with some feline features. It is primarily gray with a long, purple tail.

  • Squirtle #006
    • Water

    Squirtles are turtle Pokémon with large eyes and chubby cheeks, capable of moving either on two feet or on all fours.

  • Caterpie #007
    • Shield

    Ninetales is a quadrupedal canine Pokémon covered in thick, luxurious golden-white fur. It has slender legs with three-toed paws and nine, long tails with pale orange tips.

  • Clefairy #008
    • Magic

    Snorlax is a very large and fat mammalian Pokémon that has grayish-blue fur and an apricot-color underbelly.
